What is PickHacks?

PickHacks is a place for exploration. You will have 48 hours to delve into your curiosities, learn something new, and make something awesome. With world-class mentors and 200+ fellow hackers, you're in for an amazing time. Whether you’re a seasoned hacker or completely new to the world of hacking, we’ll have something for you. If you're ready for an adventure, we'll see you by the campfire!

Your questions, answered.

Who can attend?

All undergraduate, graduate, and high school students from any school are welcome. If you are under 18, we’ll need a parental consent form.

I don’t have a team. Can I still participate?

Of course! It is absolutely not necessary to have a team prior to coming. We’ll have some fun team-forming sessions at the beginning where you can find an awesome group of people to work with. You’re welcome to have up to four people on a team.

What should I bring?

Your valid student ID, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want (we'll be providing air mattresses), and lots of excitement!

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries!

How can I get there?

Car, bus, train, plane -- however you want. We’ll also be sending busses to select schools around the Midwest.

Is there travel reimbursement?

We’re currently working on allocating our budget to enable as many people to attend as possible. Stay tuned and we’ll let you know closer to the event date.
